About Us - RetroFighters

Welcome to Retro Fighters Arcade! We are a group of passionate students on a mission to bring back the magic of classic gaming. Our goal is to design and build a fully functional retro arcade machine from scratch, using components that we are readily available to us.

Our team is dedicated to creating an authentic arcade experience while learning valuable skills in hardware and software design. From designing the cabinet to wiring the controls, we're excited to share our journey and bring our love for retro gaming to life!

Meet the Team

Team Name: RetroFighters

Project: Building a Retro Arcade Machine

Each of us brings something unique to the table, from programming and electronics to game design and creativity. Together, we're combining our skills to build an awesome arcade that will let you relive the golden days of gaming!
