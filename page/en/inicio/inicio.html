Gran Turismo

Gran Turismo is a driving simulator developed for the PlayStation 1. Gran Turismo was developed by Poliphony Digital and published by Sony Computer Entertainment in 1999. It is the sequel to Gran Turismo 1.

Super Mario Bros

It is a platform video game developed and published in 1985 by Nintendo for the Famicom in Japan and for the Nintendo Entertainment System in North America. It is the successor to the 1983 arcade game Mario Bros and the first in the Super Mario series.

Sonic The Hedgehog

Sonic The Hedgehog is a platform video game developed by the Sonic Team and published by Sega for the home console Sega Mega Drive (also known as the Sega Genesis) in 1991. It went on sale in North America on June 23 and in the regions PAL and Japan the following month.

Crash Bandicoot

It is a platform video game developed by Naughty Dog and published by Sony Computer Entertainment for the PlayStation in 1996. The player controls Crash, a genetically enhanced bandicoot created by mad scientist Doctor Neo Cortex.
